📍 AMC — Amsterdam UMC

Meibergdreef 9, 1105 AZ Amsterdam Zuidoost. South-east edge of the city, Watergraafsmeer / Duivendrecht area, near the A10 ring road and Holendrecht metro station.

📍 Google Maps 🚌 9292 Journey planner GVB transit info
🚶 Walkable neighbourhoods
Watergraafsmeer 10–18 min
Quiet, green, residential. Best rental supply near AMC.
Duivendrecht ~15 min
Closest to hospital. Very practical, low-key vibe.
Amstel / Omval 20–25 min
Charming canal character. Slightly pricier.
Amsterdam Oost 1 bus
Vibrant, diverse. Bus 40 or 65 straight to AMC.
🚌 Routes to AMC
Bus 40 — Centraal → Oost → AMC. ~35 min. Most direct from inner city.
Bus 65 — Wibautstraat (Oost) south to AMC. ~20 min from Oost hotels.
Metro 54 — Gein line to Holendrecht, then Bus 40 or 15 min walk.
Bike — from Oost 15–25 min on dedicated lanes. OV-fiets at stations.
